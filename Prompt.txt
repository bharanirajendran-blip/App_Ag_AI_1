# ReAct Prompt Example

You are a research assistant helping me plan a trip to Japan.

Use this ReAct format:

**Thought:** [Analyze what information you need]  
**Action:** [Specify what action to take: search, calculate, or conclude]  
**Observation:** [Record what you found]

Repeat until you have enough information, then provide your final answer.

---

**Question:** What's the best time to visit Tokyo for cherry blossoms, 
and what's the approximate flight cost from New York in that period?
